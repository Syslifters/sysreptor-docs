    Alternatives to Serpico Pentesting Reporting Tool

    Similar projects and and alternatives to Serpico Penetration Test Reporting Tool.


    Name Pros Cons Report Customization Costs/User/Month
    SysReptor Fastest to get started, Tailored to pentesting needs Limited permission management in free tier HTML/CSS/VueJS Free or € 70
    AttackForge Collaboration features Unintuitive project dashboard docx with customized template tags Free or $ 30 to $ 50
    Dradis Activity feed and audit trail of reporting activity Cumbersome customizing in MS Word docx (Dradis optionally customizes for you) Free or $ 79 or $ 149
    Hexway Hive Lots of additional features Rather overloaded for reporting requirements docx with jinja-like syntax (Hexway customizes for you) Free or $ 78
    PlexTrac Large set of predefined finding templates Intransparent product presentation docx/Jinja2 Top secret
    Pwndoc Easy and focused on pentest reporting Cumbersome image upload docx via docxtemplater Free and Open Source
    Reconmap Intuitive, fast results Limited report customization (no conditials, images, etc) docx via PHPWord Free and Open Source
    WriteHat Focused on pentest reporting, from pentesters for pentesters Based on outdated Django version HTML/Django Templating Language Free and Open Source

    This overview of penetration testing reporting tools has been compiled to the best of our knowledge and belief. We do not guarantee that the information is correct or up-to-date.

    We regard software projects without updates for one year or with missing security patches as discontinued.
