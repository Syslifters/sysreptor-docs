    Alternatives to PeTeReport Pentesting Reporting Tool

    Similar projects and and alternatives to PeTeReport Penetration Test Reporting Tool.

    SysReptor is a Pentest Reporting Tool written by pentesters, for pentesters. It is built with security in mind, best usability and strongest focus on the needs of pentesters.

    However, if it does not fit your needs, here is a list of alternative tools.


    Name Report Customization Deployment Costs/User/Month

    AttackForge docx with customized template tags Cloud or OnPrem (Enterprise only) Free or $ 30 to $ 50
    Canopy docx with custom Word plugin Cloud/OnPrem Unknown
    Dradis docx (Dradis optionally customizes for you) Cloud/OnPrem Free or $ 79 or $ 149
    Faraday docx/Jinja2 Cloud/OnPrem Free or from $ 120
    Ghostwriter docx/Jinja2 OnPrem Free and Open Source
    Hexway Hive docx with jinja-like syntax (Hexway customizes for you) Cloud/OnPrem Free or $ 78
